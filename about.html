Our Foundation

Mission & Vision

Our Mission
Delivering Mining Solutions That Last

To provide integrated, safe, and sustainable mining services that unlock the potential of mineral resources across Southern Africa — through technical excellence, disciplined project execution, and a people-first culture that builds lasting value for our clients, communities, and employees.

Our Vision
Africa's Premier Mining Services Partner

To be the most trusted and capable mining services group in Africa — recognised for operational excellence, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to safety, quality, and environmental stewardship across every project we undertake.

Our Journey

Built For Excellence

From humble beginnings to a multi-division group serving the continent's most demanding mining operations.

Partner With Us
Foundation
Established as a Mining Services Provider
Mandfar Group was established with a clear mandate: deliver integrated, world-class mining services across Southern Africa with integrity and technical excellence.
Early Growth
Expansion into Construction & Civils
Recognising the synergies between mining and infrastructure, the Group launched Mandfar Construction, extending capabilities into earthworks, roads and civils.
Diversification
Electro-Mechanical & Energy Division Launched
Mandfar Electro was established to address growing demand for power infrastructure and engineering solutions in the mining sector, including solar PV and substations.
ISO Certification
Triple ISO Certification Achieved
Mandfar Group achieved ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001 certifications, cementing its position as a world-class, compliant mining services partner.
Today
Delivering Across Southern Africa
With 500+ skilled professionals, a strong asset base, and proven project delivery across gold, lithium, chrome and energy sectors, Mandfar Group continues to grow.
