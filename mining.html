Mining Excellence

Mandfar Contract Mining is the operational backbone of the Mandfar Group, delivering comprehensive open-cast mining services across Southern Africa's most demanding mineral operations.

Mining Services

Open-Cast Contract Mining

Full-service open-cast mining operations including planning, scheduling, and execution across various ore types and geological conditions.

Load & Haul Operations
