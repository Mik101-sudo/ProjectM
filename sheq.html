Our Framework

The SHEQ Framework

Four pillars that define how we operate — non-negotiable standards embedded in every aspect of our work.

S
Safety
Zero Harm Culture

Safety is not a priority that can be traded off — it is a core value. We believe all injuries and occupational illnesses are preventable, and we work tirelessly to eliminate hazards at source.

Zero fatality and zero injury target on all sites
Daily toolbox talks and hazard identification
Stop-work authority for all employees
Incident investigation and corrective action
Personal Protective Equipment enforcement
H
Health
Wellbeing First

Occupational health is a fundamental right. We proactively manage health risks including dust, noise, vibration, and chemical exposures to protect our workforce.

Regular occupational health surveillance
Respiratory and hearing protection programmes
Fatigue management and wellness support
HIV/AIDS and chronic disease management
First aid and emergency response capability
E
Environment
ISO 14001 Certified

We operate with a deep respect for the natural environment. Our ISO 14001-certified Environmental Management System ensures we minimise our footprint and comply with all regulations.

Environmental impact assessments on all projects
Dust suppression and water management
Waste minimisation and recycling programmes
Progressive rehabilitation planning
Biodiversity and community impact monitoring
Q
Quality
ISO 9001 Certified

Quality is built into everything we do — from project planning and execution to final handover. Our ISO 9001-certified Quality Management System guarantees consistent delivery.

Documented quality management procedures
Independent QA/QC inspection and testing
Non-conformance tracking and resolution
Client satisfaction measurement
Continuous improvement through data analysis
